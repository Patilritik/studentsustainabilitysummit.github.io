Sustainability For All

University of Maryland College Park Student Government Association's Sustainability Committee hosts this student-led event that serves as a platform where students from any background, location, and knowledge level have an equitable opportunity to learn about sustainability in various fields, including technology, science, education, policy, law, art, psychology, and more.

Goals (4Es)

  1. Enable equitable education on sustainability.
  2. Expand sustainability network.
  3. Expose pervasive nature and interconnectedness of sustainability.
  4. Encourage the minds of the future leaders in sustainability.

All students are welcome

This event is free to students from high schools, community colleges, and universities. The virtual portion is targeted towards students across the globe. The in-person portion is targeted towards students within commuting distance of the University of Maryland College Park campus.

Become part of the team

We are looking for students to help with making this event a success. You can help with planning, outreach, moderation, speaker recruitment, website development, and more! If you are interested in becoming a volunteer, email University of Maryland Student Government Association's Sustainability Commitee.

Join us in-person or virtually (or both). We can't wait to see you there!
